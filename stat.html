Statistics

The Statistics panel provides a visual and analytical view of sales, vehicle admissions, and operational performance across the TicketPro system. It allows administrators and managers to quickly identify trends, measure growth, and evaluate efficiency over time.

Types of Statistics

TicketPro generates multiple categories of statistical insights:

  • Daily Overview: Summary of tickets sold, vehicles admitted, and payments processed on a given day.
  • Monthly Comparison: Tracks month-to-month sales growth and vehicle admission trends.
  • Yearly Growth: Displays long-term revenue progression and system utilization rates.
  • Entity Breakdown: Separates data by individual entityâ€™s to show localized performance.

Visualization

Graphs and charts such as line graphs are used to present data in an easy-to-understand format. Administrators can switch between views (daily, monthly, yearly) for clearer insights.

Filters and Controls

To refine insights, the Statistics page includes filters:

  • Date Range: Analyze performance within a specific time frame.
  • Entity Selector: View stats from a single entity or aggregate across all entity.
  • Operator: Break down results by operator activity and contributions.

Use Cases

The Statistics feature supports multiple use cases:

  • Management can measure progress against targets.
  • Administrators can identify peak periods of activity.
  • Finance teams can analyze revenue flow across payment options (cash vs POS).
  • Operational teams can adjust resources based on vehicle inflow/outflow trends.

Purpose

By consolidating data into interactive reports, the Statistics page gives decision-makers the ability to track performance in real-time, improve planning, and enhance overall system accountability.
