Announcement

The Announcement panel allows administrators and managers to post, view, and manage notices across the TicketPro system. This ensures important information reaches the right audience at the right time.

Posting Announcements

Administrators can post announcements either as General Notices (visible to all entity's) or target specific entity by selecting them from the drop-down menu. Managers, on the other hand, can only post announcements within their assigned entity.

Viewing Announcements

All users can see announcements that are relevant to their scope. Each notice displays the content, posting entity, author, and date/time for accountability.

Deleting Announcements

Announcements can be removed either by administrators or by the original author who created them. This ensures both control and flexibility in communication management.

Purpose

The Announcement feature creates a reliable communication channel within TicketPro, reducing confusion and ensuring that all staff stay updated with critical information, policy changes, and alerts.
