Daily Sales

The Daily Sales module provides a detailed breakdown of all ticket transactions within a specific entity. It allows administrators and managers to review sales records, apply filters, and view totals for each day as well as an overall summary.

Entity Selection

Administrators can view sales across all entity, while Managers and Park Officers are restricted to their assigned entity. The entity filter ensures that users only see the data relevant to their role.

Filtering Options

Users can refine the displayed records using:

  • Operator Filter: View sales handled by a specific entry operator.
  • Payment Filter: Narrow results by Entry Cash, Entry POS, Exit Cash, or Exit POS.
A clear filter button resets all filters to default.

Sales Records

Each transaction is displayed with the following details:

  • Receipt ID – Unique transaction number.
  • Sale Time – Exact timestamp of the ticket sale.
  • Ticket Type – Category of ticket sold.
  • Entry Operator – The staff who issued the ticket.
  • Payment Method – Whether paid via cash or POS at entry.
  • Exit Payment – Method recorded at exit (if applicable).
  • Extra Fees – Additional charges applied.
  • Ticket Price – Base amount for the ticket.

Daily Totals

For each day, the system calculates and displays:

  • Extra Fee Total – Sum of all additional charges.
  • Ticket Total – Sum of all ticket prices.
  • Day Grand Total – Combined ticket and extra fee totals.

Overall Summary

At the bottom of the page, an overall summary highlights the Total Ticket Sales, Total Extra Fees, and Grand Total for the current day across the selected market.

Purpose

The Daily Sales feature ensures transparency and accountability in revenue tracking. By grouping sales by date and allowing filters by operator and payment method, it gives management a clear and actionable financial overview.
